One real semantic-recall run proving the live `OpenAICompatEmbedder` gives meaningful ranking (vs the

## Setup
Indexed 5 diverse memories: favourite colour (teal), building Mnemosyne, peanut allergy, lives in
Berlin, favourite food (sushi).

## Results — every query's top-1 is the semantically correct memory
| Query | Top-1 | score | unrelated next |

| What am I allergic to? | "I am allergic to peanuts." | 0.7750 | sushi 0.52 |

real semantic ranking. This is the quality lever recall needed at scale (HashEmbedder only worked
because top-k returned all of a tiny set).
